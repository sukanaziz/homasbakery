Order Policy

Order Confirmation

All orders submitted through our website are considered requests and are not confirmed until you receive confirmation from our team by phone or email.

Deposit Requirement

All confirmed orders require a 50% non-refundable prepayment. This deposit secures your order date and preparation.

Pickup & Delivery

Orders may be scheduled for pickup or delivery. Delivery availability may be subject to location and additional fees.

Delivery Fees

Delivery orders may include an additional fee depending on distance. Final delivery cost will be confirmed during the order confirmation process.

Cancellations

Prepayments are non-refundable once an order has been confirmed. Changes or cancellations may not be guaranteed depending on preparation status.

Product Availability

All orders are subject to product availability. We reserve the right to make adjustments if certain items are unavailable.

Contact

For questions about your order, please contact us at homasbakery20@gmail.com or send us a direct message on our instagram homasbakery.
